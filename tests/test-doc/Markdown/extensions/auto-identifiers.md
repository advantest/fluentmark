### [Extension: `auto_identifiers`](https://pandoc.org/MANUAL.html#extension-auto_identifiers)

Headings and sections automatically get an identifier that can be used in references 
/ links.
The headings are transformed to lower case letters, spaces and new lines are 
removed, all non-alphanumeric characters are remove except of
underscores, hyphens, and periods.
For example, the title "Headings and sections" is translated to the identifier
`headings-and-sections`.
